Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is FlashWash?

FlashWash is a subscription-based car wash platform that allows you to wash your car at any of our partner locations across South Africa.

❓ How does the subscription work?

Choose a plan (Basic, Standard, or Premium), pay monthly, and access washes at any registered partner location.

❓ Where can I use my subscription?

You can use your plan at any FlashWash-approved partner car wash listed on our platform.

❓ How do I become a partner car wash?

Simply fill out the partner registration form and our team will get in touch with you.

❓ Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time without penalties.

❓ How do I contact FlashWash?

Email us at support@flashwash.co.za or fill out the contact form on our website.
